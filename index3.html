Asrkari Satyagraha

गांधीवादी सिद्धांतों और दर्शन के अनुरूप एक स्थायी, सर्वांगीण और अहिंसक अर्थव्यवस्था के लिए एक अहिंसक आंदोलन। इसका उद्देश्य कोरोनावायरस महामारी के प्रतिकूल प्रभावों को कम करना है जो संभावित रूप से अत्यधिक गरीबी, बढ़ती लैंगिक असमानता और मानव अधिकारों के उल्लंघन में वृद्धि की ओर जाता है।

ASRKARI
A-srkāri and Asr-kāri – the name can be pronounced in two different ways. Each way is a combination of two words with different meanings. The word A-srkari means non-governmental, i.e. community driven, and Asr-kari means effective.

SATYAGRAHA
Satyagraha is a composition of two words from Sanskrit, satya (truth) and āgraha (insistence), and translates to “holding onto truth”. It is a particular form of non-violent resistance or civil resistance which advocates resisting non-violently over using violence. Resisting non-violently is considered the summit of bravery. The term was coined and developed by Mahatma Gandhi. He deployed satyagraha in the Indian independence movement and also during his earlier struggles in South Africa for Indian rights.
